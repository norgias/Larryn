I Started Larryn's Lending hand as a way to help homeless people in Atlanta by handing out food, and clothes. Since we July of 2014 we have successfully giving back to the community downtown.

Who I Am

I was born and raised in Phoenix Arizona and moved to Atlanta back in 2012. Upon arriving here I fell in love with the city. That being said however, I knew that I wanted to somehow give back to the community by helping the homeless downtown. It was then that Larryn's Lending Hand was born.
