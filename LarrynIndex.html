What I Do

I Started Larryn's Lending hand as a way to help the homeless and less fortunate in Atlanta by handing out food and clothes. As of 2014 we have been successfully giving back to the community.

Who I Am

I was born and raised in Phoenix Arizona and moved to Atlanta back in 2012. Upon arriving here I fell in love with the city. That being said however, I knew that I wanted to somehow give back to the community by helping the homeless in downtown Atlanta. It was then that Larryn's Lending Hand was born.
